SOPs & Workflows
STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES · WEEKLY RHYTHM
SOP 01 — Sunday Service Setup
▼
1
Arrive 90 min before service. Set up Camera A at center, Camera B at side for worship coverage.
Camera Team
2
Test all microphones. Run audio check with worship team. Confirm recording levels.
Audio Lead
3
Load ProPresenter: worship lyrics, announcements, lower thirds, offering slides.
ProPresenter Op
4
Run 2-min test stream. Confirm stream receiving, thumbnail set, title updated.
Livestream Lead
5
Team prayer — 5 min before doors open. Align in the spirit before execution begins.
All Team
SOP 02 — Content Posting Workflow
▼
1
Post-service: Director identifies 3 clip moments and logs them with timestamps in Content Pipeline.
Director
2
Editor cuts clips by Monday EOD. Uploads to shared drive labeled [Date — Clip Type].
Editor
3
Director reviews and approves. Writes or approves captions. Tags platforms.
Director
4
Social lead schedules posts. Full sermon uploaded to YouTube with SEO-optimized description.
Social Lead
Weekly Execution Flow
▼
M
Monday — Reset & Brief
Review Sunday wrap. Update Spiritual Alignment. Send weekly brief to team. Assign all content and tasks.
T
Tuesday — Content Day
Sermon clips go live. Editor delivers cuts. Director reviews and approves. Update pipeline board.
W
Wednesday — Midweek + Check-In
Midweek service coverage. Mid-week team check-in. Review Sunday prep status.
T
Thursday — Production Prep
Second content post. Begin Sunday pre-production checklist. Confirm team assignments.
F
Friday — Final Prep
Send team Saturday reminder. All Sunday graphics due. Equipment check.
✦
Sunday — Execute + Wrap
Pre-service setup (90 min early). Team prayer. Full production. Post-service wrap: footage offload, clip ID, debrief.
